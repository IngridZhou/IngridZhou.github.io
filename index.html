My Journey

I used to think that the interesting thing was that stars come from stars hundreds of millions of light-years away, so every time we look at the sky, we see what happened hundreds of millions of years through time.

Later, I found that the real interesting thing was not that we saw the past, but that the stars wrapped in history and landed in the moment of our life through the infinite interstellar space and time.

Not only the stars, but every moment of our life is the most unique point. Everything in the universe comes from every corner of time and space, from the world, from the past, from the future.

Everything for hundreds of millions of years blossoms in this unique moment and point at the same time. In this way, any moment of anyone is the unique and supreme moment in the universe.

We are the home of the stars.
